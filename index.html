What is Studyrooms?

A place for working together

Video call your peers with simultaneous document editing!

A platform that provides a comprehensive solution for collaborative brainstorming, emphasizing community interaction, content moderation, and diverse user interfaces. Clear and simply formatted allowing easy navigation for all. Creating a safe and engaging environment for everyone.

Why use StudyRooms?

Seamless collaboration anyone can use!

We focus on a engaging user experience, ensuring immersion in the workspace.

Studyrooms is the perfect tool for working with your school peers or work colleagues, from anywhere you want!

Start collaborating Today!

Features

Easily create, import and edit documents collaboratively with your classmates, co-workers and peers using our online text editor.

All of your documents can be stored in the StudyRooms cloud storage, easily accessed through your account and shared with your peers. Start with 10GB cloud storage for free!

Message a peer directly or invite your peers to a group chat to be able to communicate through text and share ideas and documents.

Innovative voice and video call features to be able to confer with peers effectively and efficiently.
